# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

CB File Hub is a local-first file manager. This app is designed to help you browse, organize, and open files on your device and on network locations that you choose to connect.

## What the app accesses

CB File Hub may access:

- Local files and folders that you open or manage
- Photos, videos, documents, and downloads that you choose to browse
- Network locations such as SMB, FTP, or WebDAV servers that you configure
- App preferences, workspace state, tags, and similar local settings
- Saved network connection details if you choose to store them in the app

## How data is used

The app uses this data only to provide core file management features, including:

- Browsing local and network files
- Opening media and generating thumbnails
- Saving tags, preferences, and workspace state
- Remembering optional network connection settings

## What the app does not do

CB File Hub does not include:

- A hosted cloud account system operated by us
- In-app advertising
- Third-party analytics or user tracking services

We do not collect your files or personal content on our own servers through the app.

## When data leaves your device

Data may be transferred over your network only when you explicitly use network features such as SMB, FTP, or WebDAV. In those cases, your device communicates with the server or share that you selected.

## Data storage

App settings, tags, cache files, and optional saved network connection details are stored locally on your device.

You can remove app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app, subject to how your operating system handles local files and caches.

## Permissions

The app may request permissions such as file storage, media access, or local network access so that its file browsing and network features can work.

You can deny or revoke permissions at any time in your device settings, although some features may stop working correctly.
